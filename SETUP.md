## Vision: The Path Forward

CrewWealth is an income planning platform designed for maritime professionals. By consolidating income from multiple sources (base salary, overtime, tips, bonuses), we empower crew members to:

Forecast Real Income - Understand earning potential across contracts
Plan Smarter - Build wealth through disciplined savings strategies
Work Globally - Support multiple currencies & contract types
Track Progress - Visualize journey to financial independence
