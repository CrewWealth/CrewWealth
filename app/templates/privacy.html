Privacy Statement

CrewWealth stores account and budgeting information in your personal user scope to deliver dashboard, goals, reports, and forecasting features.

What we store

Your profile details, financial records you create, and app preferences.

How access is restricted

Authentication is required and Firestore rules are configured so users can only read and write documents within their own users/{uid} path.
